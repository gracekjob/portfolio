‘M+ Sigg Collection: Four Decades of Chinese Contemporary Art’ – the first-ever chronological exhibition of the emergence of Chinese contemporary art at ArtisTree, Hong Kong.

M+ Sigg Collection app is the digital guide to enhance the exhibition experience. Use it to learn about the art in front of user, hear from the artists and M+ curators, and keep a record of user's favourite artworks to view at home.
