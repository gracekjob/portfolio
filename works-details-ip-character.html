Discuss - IP Characters

Discuss is an online discussion platform for Hong Kong people.With more than 5.5 million registered members who came from different classes and background.

In 2016, Discuss was voted to be one of the Hong Kong most popular 10 websites in consecutive 3 years.

As one of the most recognizable brands in Hong Kong, the brand has suffered from limited customer base, members of Discuss are mainly adult and working class. In order to attract younger members, Discuss implements rejuvenation by building up its visual identity: Discuss Family.
